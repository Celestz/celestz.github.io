List of Contenders

MSI Evolution Gaming Team

Few teams can boast the same rich history as the MSi Evolution Gaming Team. MSi was one of the first true professional League of Legends teams in the Philippines. Back when they were known as Team WILD, they stood tall as the best team in the country, and fought for the country's name internationally as well. But since then, MSi has suffered a fall from grace, and are trying to claw back to relevance while being led by one of the best players in the country, their veteran captain: Karlo.

Medyo Bad Girl

Many think of the world of eSports to be a male dominated field. While they're not entirely wrong, Erika and her team, Medyo Bad Girl, would like to sway your views. Originating from the original Philippine all-girl team, MBG's captain envisions her team as a powerhouse of femme fatales capable of keeping up with the best of the boys. Never one to run out of cards to play, for every Jack of all trades, MBG has a Queen ready to do them one better.

Redemption

There are two sides to every coin. And on the coin of the now defunct Team Goddess, once a very prominent all-girls team, one side of it is Team Redemption. Created by the original makers of Team Goddess, these women defected from the group they once called family due to internal turmoil and are now hellbent on redeeming themselves and shed their tarnished image. Helping them along the way is their "small" secret who always makes very big plays.

Defiance

After the split of what is now Team Redemption from Goddess, the ones who stayed looked to rebuild both their roster, and identity. Now known as Team Defiance, they look to defy the odds and become their own bright luster. Though some are still angels earning their wings, the team, led by Cairi Jing, draws upon team synergy to fuel their desire to win. Don't be caught off guard, because behind the face is a fighter who fights to succeed.

Rainbow Kitties

They say looks can be deceiving. They didn't say looks can also kick your butt. The Rainbow Kitties might seem unassuming and innocent, but they have been causing waves across the LoLPH community as being one of the strongest all-girls team. Leading the way is the consensus number one female player in the Philippines: RKT Black. It might seem like RKT is always playing just for fun. But there's nothing they enjoy more than beating the pulp out of everyone in their way. When it's go time, RKT can turn from cute to cut-throat with a flip of a switch.

Imperium Pro Team

From humble beginnings to living like rock stars, the members of the Imperium Pro Team have been at the top since their inception. Having once been the sponsorless, yet dominant Team Exile, IPT has now become a household name among League of Legends enthusiasts in the Philippines. Featuring a stacked roster, the team keeps skyrocketing and doesn't seem to have any plans of slowing down. For now, they remain unstoppable.
